01 — Brand Foundation

What Sarbat
stands for

Every decision — name, design, product, marketing — flows from these core truths.

01 / Name Origin
Sarbat da Bhala
From the Sikh Ardas prayer — "wellbeing and prosperity for all of humanity." This prayer has been recited for 500 years across every caste, creed, and community. It belongs to no one exclusively. It belongs to everyone. Exactly like your platform.
02 / Mission
Every business deserves great tools
India's 63 million small businesses run on grit, chai, and WhatsApp forwards. They have been ignored by every enterprise software company. Sarbat exists to change that — one shop, one salon, one clinic at a time.
03 / Vision
The operating system for Indian SMEs
A widely adopted conversational business infrastructure enabling millions of small businesses to operate digitally — in their own language, on a platform they already know: WhatsApp.
04 / Core Promise
Simple. Direct. Zero Commission.
No app installs for customers. No commissions on orders. No learning curve for merchants. Just a WhatsApp bot that makes every small business feel like it has an entire operations team.
05 / Brand Personality
The trusted neighbourhood expert
Warm but capable. Street-smart but reliable. Proudly Indian but globally ready. Sarbat speaks to a 60-year-old shopkeeper and a 25-year-old startup founder in the same breath — and both feel seen.
06 / Positioning
Anti-Swiggy. Anti-Amazon.
Where every other platform takes a cut, charges for installs, or complexifies operations — Sarbat does the opposite. It makes the merchant the centre of gravity. Direct commerce. Full ownership. Zero friction.

03 — Brand Story

The Story We Tell

India has 63 million small businesses.

They run grocery stores and salons. They fix your shoes and file your taxes. They cook your tiffin and teach your children. They are the real economy of this country.

Yet every tool built for "business" was built for someone else. Too expensive. Too complex. In the wrong language. Requiring hardware they can't afford or apps their customers won't install.

Meanwhile, everyone already has WhatsApp.

A kirana owner in Coimbatore gets 40 availability enquiries a day on WhatsApp. A tailor in Lucknow loses orders because he can't track them. A salon owner in Patna has no idea which service is her bestseller.

They don't need Salesforce. They don't need a POS machine. They need something that works — right where they already are.

That's Sarbat. Sab ke liye.

07 — Go-to-Market

How Sarbat
reaches India

A phased approach that starts street-level and scales to every corner of the subcontinent.

1
Plant the Flag
Launch sarbat.io with a waitlist. Founder-led content on LinkedIn and Instagram telling the story of why Sarbat exists. Target Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities first — that's where the need is most acute and competition is thinnest.
🎯 Target: 10,000 waitlist signups
2
Community First
Partner with local trade associations, mandis, and SME WhatsApp groups. Run free onboarding workshops in regional languages. Every new merchant gets a QR code starter kit for their shop window. Word of mouth is the growth engine.
🎯 Target: First 500 paying SMEs
3
Proof of Impact
Document 10 deep SME success stories — filmed, real, emotional. Video testimonials in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Marathi, Bengali. Push earned media with the angle: "The startup helping India's invisible businesses." Build credibility for the next phase.
🎯 Target: Press coverage + investor interest
4
Scale the Network
Launch referral program — "Apne dost ko bhi Sarbat do." Channel partners in every state. Vernacular ad campaigns targeting regional SME groups. The discovery portal creates organic SEO that brings customers to merchants, which brings merchants to Sarbat.
🎯 Target: 10,000 active SMEs
5
Beyond India
WhatsApp-dominant markets — Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa — face identical problems. The Sarbat model travels perfectly. "Sarbat da Bhala" resonates deeply in Muslim-majority markets where the prayer concept is familiar. Same product, new geographies.
🎯 Target: First international market live
