03 — Brand Story

The story
we tell.

There is a shop on almost every street in India.

A grocery store. A cutting chai stall. A tailor. A repair shop. A small restaurant that has been feeding the same neighbourhood for twenty years.

These businesses run on relationships, reputation, and hard work. They run on the owner's memory, a handwritten khata, and 40 WhatsApp messages asking if tomatoes are available.

They are the backbone of India's economy. And yet — every business tool ever built was built for someone else. Too expensive. Too complex. Too English. Too foreign.

We thought that was wrong.

So we built Pakoda. A WhatsApp bot you set up in minutes. A catalog your customers browse on their phone. Orders that come in automatically. Inventory that tracks itself. All in your language. No app installs. Zero commissions. Ever.

Because a kirana owner in Thrissur shouldn't need a tech degree to run his business well. Because a salon owner in Patna deserves the same tools as a chain salon in Bangalore.

Because desi business
deserves a desi platform.
04 — Marketing Campaigns

Six campaigns that
build Pakoda.

Each campaign targets a different emotion, audience, or growth lever — together they cover the full funnel from awareness to activation.

01
Chai Pe Charcha
The chai-time conversation
Launch Campaign
Short, warm documentary-style films shot at real tea stalls and small shops. A grocer, a tailor, a tiffin service owner — all talking about their biggest business headache while having chai. Then they discover Pakoda. The transition is not magical or dramatic — it's practical and human. "Arre, yeh toh kaafi aasaan hai." That's the emotion.
"Har chhoti dukaan ka bada sapna hota hai.
Ab uske liye ek bada platform bhi hai."
60-sec mini documentaries Instagram Reels YouTube pre-roll Regional language cuts WhatsApp viral sharing
02
Pehla Order
The first order
Emotional / Viral
The moment a small business owner receives their first WhatsApp order through Pakoda — that notification sound, that feeling of disbelief and joy. Capture this moment with real merchants across India. A flower seller in Bengaluru. A pharmacist in Indore. A home baker in Lucknow. That first order changes everything. Film it. Share it. Make it a movement.
"Pehla order. Aur baaki sab badal gaya."
#PehlaOrder UGC campaign Merchant reaction videos Instagram Stories Free trial trigger on tag
03
Zero Commission. Zero Tension.
Ek bhi paisa nahi katenge
Performance / Conversion
A direct, honest comparison. Show an interactive calculator — enter your monthly revenue, see how much Swiggy, Zomato, or Amazon takes versus how much Pakoda costs. The math is brutal and obvious. Make the merchant angry at the commission model, then offer Pakoda as the clear, rational alternative. Performance ads targeting existing Swiggy/Zomato merchants.
"Wo 30% lete hain. Hum zero.
Tumhari kamai. Sirf tumhari."
Interactive commission calculator Performance ads Google search targeting Retargeting campaign
04
Pakoda Challenge
Tag. Share. Grow.
Viral Growth
Challenge every Indian to post a photo with their favourite local shop, tag them, and say why they love them using #PakodaChallenge. Every tagged business gets a direct outreach from Pakoda with a 30-day free trial. Creates massive organic reach, a warm lead pipeline, and a community of local business champions — all at once.
"Tag your favourite local business.
We'll help them grow."
#PakodaChallenge Instagram + Twitter Auto outreach to tagged merchants PR story opportunity
05
Aap Ki Bhasha
In your language
Regional Expansion
Show Pakoda working in Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, Punjabi, Malayalam, and Hindi — simultaneously on the same screen. A split-screen product demo that shows one platform speaking every language. Run separate, localised campaigns in each state with region-specific merchants and regional-language content. Each state gets its own campaign version.
"Pakoda speaks your language.
Every Indian language."
State-specific ad campaigns Vernacular landing pages Regional influencer collabs Local trade association tie-ups

Dukaan Wali Didi / Bhaiya
The shopkeeper who made it

A long-running series following 12 real merchants — one per month — across India. Deep-dive storytelling. Their life, their business, their struggle, their growth with Pakoda. Not an ad — a genuine human story. Monthly editorial content that builds deep brand equity, emotional loyalty, and earned media over time. The Pakoda Merchant Stories series.

One Pakoda."

10 — Founder Positioning

How you show up
as founder.

Your Story
"I didn't build this for Bangalore. I built it for the shop next door."
Your founding story is personal. You saw a business — maybe in your own family, neighbourhood, or city — that was running on grit and WhatsApp messages and had no real tools. That frustration is your credibility. Lead with it every time.
Your Positioning
The founder who chose the 63 million over the 1%.
Every other startup chases premium customers. You went the other direction — deliberately. That contrarian choice is powerful and resonates deeply with press, investors who care about impact, and the merchants themselves.
What You Post
5 content pillars for your personal brand.
1. SME stories — businesses you've met and their reality.
2. Build in public — weekly honest updates on Pakoda's progress.
3. India SME data — insights that make people think.
4. Mistakes made — vulnerability builds trust faster than wins.
5. Why zero commission matters — educate the market.
Your Signature Line
"Every time someone eats a pakoda in India, I want them to think of their business."
This is your closer. End interviews with it. Put it in your Twitter bio. It's funny, warm, true, and impossible to forget. It also makes every journalist smile — which means they write about you.
